Better Potions adds unique potions, modifies vanilla potions, and adds a few quality of life changes!

[h2]TLDR[/h2]

- Adds 13 new potions
- Buffs 6 vanilla potions
- Makes (most) potions last 10 minutes and allows them to persist after death
- Multiplayer is supported
- Highly configurable
- Currently supports English and Russian, see below if you'd like to contribute another translation

[h2]Vanilla Potion Changes[/h2]

- Ammo Reservation - 50% (was 20%) chance to not consume ammo
- Archery - Has been turned into the Predator Potion, 10% increased ranged damage and critical chance
- Builder - Increased placement range by 3 (was 1) tiles and increased placement speed by 50% (was 25%)
- Mana Regeneration - Also grants a constant 4 mana regeneration per second which happens even if you’re using a magic weapon
- Obsidian Skin - Also makes you immune to cursed inferno and ichor
- Summoning - Also grants +1 sentry slot and increases summoning damage by 10%

[h2]Quality of Life Changes[/h2]

- Buffs are persistent after death with a penalty of 25% of the remaining time
- Most potions now last 10 minutes. Potions that lasted longer haven’t been changed and Luck potions also haven’t been changed
- Some potions and buffs have had their tooltips changed to be more informative and consistent

[h2]Configuration[/h2]

- Whether buffs are persistent after death and how much of the remaining time is lost
- Whether most potions last 10 minutes, including a blacklist
- Whether vague potions and buffs have tooltips changed
- Toggle for each vanilla potion change
- Toggle for each added potion
